K here's how to make pumpkin chocolate chip muffins. I think they're the best, so you should make them.

  • 15 ounces canned pumpkin
  • 1/2 cup salted butter (melted)
  • 3/4 cup light brow sugar
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon pumpkin pie spice
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

It'll take 30-40 minutes in total, 10 minutes of prep time and 20-25 minutes of cook time. This recipe will make 14 servings.

Instructions
Step number What you need What you do
1 Oven, muffin tin. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees and grease the muffin tin.
2 Flour, pumpkin pie spice, cinnamon, baking powder, salt, small bowl. Combine ingredients in small bowl and set aside.
3 Canned pumpkin, melted butter, large bowl. Whisk in large bowl until smooth.
4 Sugars. Add and whisk until dissolved.
5 Eggs, vanilla. Whisk until smooth.
6 Both ingredient mixtures. Slowly add dry mixture to wet mixture and stir until smooth.
7 Batter, chocolate chips. Add desired amount of chocolate chips and stir.
8 Batter, muffin tin. Pour 1/4 cup of batter into each well and smooth out the top.
9 Muffin tin, oven. Bake for 20-25 minutes until a fork comes out cleanly.
10 The muffins. Let cool and enjoy!

Tips and tricks if you care that much:

  1. Make sure the butter is properly melted.
  2. Sift and spoon the flour.
  3. Don't rush it.
