Chocolate Chip Cookies!

Here's what you're gonna be making:

Blah blah blah blah my entire life story and a quote that was definetely said, we don't care. Here's how to make cookies.

Here's what you need

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups flour (give or take)
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt (if using unsalted butter)
  • 2 cups of chocolate chips (roughly)

Now that you've got your stuff, here are your steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 and put the butter out to soften.
  2. Beat the butter and both sugars together in a large bowl.
  3. Add eggs one at a time and beat in.
  4. Add and mix in vanilla.
  5. Mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt together in a medium bowl.
  6. Slowly combine dry mixture with wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
  7. Add chocolate chips and any other mix-ins.
  8. Put parchement paper over a baking sheet and scoop cookie dough onto it, leaving a little space.
  9. Put into oven and let bake until golden brown, around 9-12 minutes.
  10. Let cool.

Why is it not working?

Issue Cause Fix
They're lumpy and didn't flatten out. The butter is likely not soft enough. Leave the butter out for longer.
They flattened out too much. The butter is likely too soft. Make sure the butter is soft, but still holds it's shape.

Those are the main issues I encounter. The recipe as written takes anywhere between 30-45 minutes, generally taking around 15 minutes to prep. It makes roughly 36 servings.
